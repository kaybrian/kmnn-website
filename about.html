KMNN (Kawalya, Mubiru, Nansubuga & Nsubuga) Solicitors & Advocates is a Law firm offering quality legal services in Uganda.

The firm comprises three active Partners and one dormant Partner. We are proactive and proficient advocates and we work as tirelessly as humanly possible to ensure that we meet our clients’ demands and expectations.

We specialize in amongst others commercial transactions, Corporate Governance and Strategic Management, Banking and Insurance, Land Law, company law, Incorporation of Companies, civil and criminal litigation, Intellectual Properties Law (Copy right, Patents and Trademarks law), Family law, as well as debt collection.

Our Partners have vast experience in legal practice. In addition to our specialized interests above, we also offer legal advice in other areas such as Mediation in commercial matters, real estate management and labour law.
