ABOUT US

We offer quality legal services in Uganda.

KMNN (Kawalya, Mubiru, Nansubuga & Nsubuga) Solicitors & Advocates is a Law firm offering quality legal services in Uganda.
We specialize in amongst others commercial transactions, Corporate Governance and Strategic Management, Banking and Insurance, Land Law, company law, Incorporation of Companies, civil and criminal litigation, Intellectual Properties Law (Copy right, Patents and Trademarks law), Family law, as well as debt collection.
