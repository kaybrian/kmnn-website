Charging of Fees

The fees charged are governed by the Advocates (Remuneration and Taxation of Costs) (amendment) Rules 2000 and the Uganda Revenue Authority’ VAT Act. We adhere to the Procedural rules in all aspects when determining our Legal fees.


In procedural matters such as litigation, actual costs to be incurred are not easily envisaged initially and so we prepare Bills of Costs in such instances. In this regard, we charge a reasonable deposit of fees which are considered when final payment of the total bill is made.

We offer two options to regular clients:-

  • Payment of an annual or monthly retainer fee which covers all matters other than litigation. This arrangement is mainly bordered on legal consultation. We require the client in this respect to pay for any disbursements that may be incurred. An agreement is drawn up to this effect;

  • We charge for work done on a piece-meal basis.
