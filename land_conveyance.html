Land Conveyance

Land Conveyance

We handle land transactions covering all aspects of land conveyance such as the registration of leases, caveats, mortgages and transfers amongst others.

We also prepare the requisite documents pertaining to transactions of the ilk such as lease/sub-lease agreements, mortgage deeds, and transfer deeds. We offer advice on the legality and efficacy of any contemplated transaction and on matters that require mandatory/statutory compliance.

We solicit for good Surveyors on behalf of our clients in the event that survey transactions are required and we effect transfers on their behalf.
