Overview

Mubiru Molly Lubowa was admitted to the bar and has been in active private practice for over five (5) years. She is an advocate of the High court and all courts surbodinate thereto. She holds a bachelors degree in law (with honours) from Makerere University and a Diploma in legal practice from Law Development Center.

Her areas of specialization are commercial transactions. She has experience in both Administrative and company law which she acquired from Posta Uganda Limited where she was employed in its Operations department and thereafter in its Legal Department as Legal Officer/Assistant to the Company Secretary for over twenty (20) years. She is knowledgeable in Family Law and Company matters.
Molly is a member of the Uganda Law Society and of the East African Law Society.
